Summer 2022

Student Research Opportunity

Student Researcher needed for a summer research project in statistical finance. The project will be related to time series forecasting and statistical machine learning, with applications to financial portfolio management. The project will either extend from previous papers, or comprise a new piece of original work, depending on the interest of the student. Students interested in either statistical research or financial portfolio construction are encouraged to apply. See below for further information.

Project Title

Portfolio Constructions and Investment Strategies

Main Supervisors

Parley Yang (Academic Side) and Bobby Previti (Industrial Side)

Period and Location of the Project

There are no strict geographical restriction on where the student is, neither a set period of time. We are looking at around 8 weeks in the summer, depending on student's interests and coding abilities.

Brief Description of Project

We shall look at optimal combination of portfolio --- this could be, for example, hedged long on US equities, systematic options trading, macroeconomic predictions and forecasts and etc. We focus on low-frequency data --- data that are available mostly in public and are updated on a daily basis. We shall develop portfolio holding strategies on tradable assets, and by the end of the period, produce an industry-level report on such strategy. There is also a direction that the student articulate the research to a more academic side, for instance, on statistical learning and financial applications.
