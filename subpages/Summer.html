Summer undergraduate research opportunity

Research assistant needed for a summer research project in statistical finance. The project will be related to time series forecasting and statistical machine learning, with applications to financial portfolio management. The project will either extend from previous papers, or comprise a new piece of original work, depending on the interest of the student. Students interested in either statistical research or financial portfolio construction are encouraged to apply.
